What Is Discord Server Boosting?

Discord server boosting is a service that increases the member count of your Discord server, making it appear more active, popular, and credible. Whether you're building a community, running a brand, or launching a project, a higher member count attracts more organic users and boosts your server's visibility.

Online Members vs Offline Members

We offer two types of member packages to fit your needs:

  • Online Members (€7 per 1,000): These members appear as "online" in your server's member list. They show a green status indicator, making your server look actively populated at all times. This is ideal for servers that want to appear busy and thriving, as potential new members will see a large number of active users when they join.
  • Offline Members (€3 per 1,000): These members are added to your server but appear as "offline." They boost your total member count displayed on your server, which increases credibility and social proof. This is a cost-effective way to inflate your server's numbers and make it look established and trusted.

  • Social Proof: A busy server looks professional and well-maintained.
  • Quick Setup: Members are delivered quickly after purchase.
  • Scalable: Order as many as you need to match your goals.
